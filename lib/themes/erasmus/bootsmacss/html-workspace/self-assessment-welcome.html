Welcome!

You have signed, on behalf of your Higher Education Institution, an Erasmus Charter for Higher Education (ECHE). This constitutes your commitment to respect in full all the principles described in this Charter and the condition for your institution to be eligible to receive European funding under the Erasmus+ programme for 2014-2020 period.

Holding the Charter gives access to numerous opportunities offered by Erasmus+. This is wider than mobility activities for students, trainees, teaching and training staff and reinforces cooperation with peers and socio-economic actors within and outside Europe. These opportunities can support your institution to increase its attractiveness, reinforce and diversify its educational offer as well as to modernise teaching and learning methodologies.

The ECHE principles are the foundation of your participation in the Erasmus+ Programme. It is vital that all 4000+ higher education institutions apply it correctly, as they all depend on each other in Erasmus+ mobility and cooperation projects. Implementing the principles contributes to the internationalisation of your institution, successful cooperation and increasing the impact of the Erasmus+ programme.

A successful implementation of the ECHE requires excellent cooperation at all levels of your institution, starting with YOU! This is a unique opportunity for you to have a comprehensive overview of your institution’s performance in implementing some of the key principles. You may also compare your institution’s results with national and European peers.

The exercise is entirely voluntary, and the results will only be visible by you. Neither the European Commission nor the National Authorities in charge of Erasmus+ in your country will have access to the results, unless you decide to share them.
